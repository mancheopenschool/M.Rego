== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this theme support any plugins? =

evision themes includes support for Infinite Scroll in Jetpack and conform 7.

== Usage ==

=== Front Page Customization ===
1. In the admin area, go to “Appearance > Customize > Front page”
1. There are everything you need to customize your awesome homepage/front page.

=== Front Page Menu ===
1. In the admin area, go to “Appearance > Menus”
2. Click “create a new menu”
3. To add Front Page Scroll Menu, click “Custom Links” and enter id of available section in 'URL' field and enter Menu title in 'Link Text' field. Click “Add to Menu”
    - Available sections id
        - #evision-corporate-slider
        - #evision-corporate-about
        - #evision-corporate-service
        - #evision-corporate-portfolio
        - #evision-corporate-client
        - #evision-corporate-testimonial
        - #evision-corporate-blog
        - #evision-corporate-team
        - #evision-corporate-map
        - #evision-corporate-contact

4. In “Theme Locations”, check “Front Page Menu”
5. Click “Save Menu”

=== Inner Pages Menu ===
1. In the admin area, go to “Appearance > Menus”
2. Click “create a new menu”
3. Add Menus
4. In “Theme Locations”, check “Inner Pages Menu”
5. Click “Save Menu”

=== Social Menu ===
1. In the admin section, go to “Appearance > Menus”
2. Click “create a new menu”
3. To add social link, click “Custom Links” and enter social link URL. Click “Add to Menu”
    - Supported social menus
        - facebook.com
        - twitter.com
        - linkedin.com
        - instagram.com

4. In “Theme Locations”, check “Social Menu”
5. Click “Save Menu”

To show menu in footer:
1. From “Customize” section, go to “Theme Options > Footer” section.
2. Turn on “Enable social icons” checkbox to show social icons.
3. Click on “Save & Publish” to save the changes.
