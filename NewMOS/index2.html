Three Tracks

At Austin Coding Academy, our job is to bring students to job-ready level proficiency. We offer two curricula for students, each one consisting of three levels: Intro, Intermediate and Advanced. Each course within a track is ten weeks and by the end of the three courses in a track students will be ready for a full-time developer position. Additionally, we offer services to produce custom curriculum and instruction for corporations with a need for technical education.

What Austin Coding Academy Means


Hands on teaching

Less lecturing, more doing. Each day, class is divided into two halves: cover and explain concepts using real-world examples and then spend the rest of class applying that new knowledge to your own projects and exercises.

Project based

Build your Github profile, the de-facto resum of the coding world. By the end of class, you'll have a solid portfolio to show off to potential employers or prospective clients.

Real-world focused

We teach using examples from real applications, products, and sites, rather than just hypotheticals. Learn skills you'll actually be using in your career moving forward.

Team oriented learning

Developers work in teams, so our curriculum is structured to build those skills from the beginning. We select students based off of their drive to learn, rather than their coding skill, allowing for an environment where students push each other to succeed.

Student-Mentor Relationship

Our instructors are passionate about their trade and are available to help whether you need some assistance catching up or you need to be pushed because you're ahead. Included in tuition is as much one-on-one tutoring as you need. 

Accountability

Our instruction is world class, but one of the hallmarks of Austin Coding Academy is the individual attention each student receives. We're dedicated to making sure every student who walks through our doors gets what they came for.

 

Career Services

Our goal at Austin Coding Academy is to bring coding to as many people as possible and within that is training and producing job-ready developers. As any veteran in the industry will tell you, cramming an education into three months isn't going to fully prepare you to be a productive member of a development team. This is why our curriculum is stretched out over three 10-week courses. Here at ACA we're about sustainable, lasting education.

As students progress through our curriculum, we have a career services department that is available to meet with students interested in turning their education into a career. As a policy we don't guarantee students jobs, as different students progress at different speeds, however we do have a dedicated staff that is committed to helping graduates find placement if and when they're ready.

The Application Process


Excited to get started? Apply here! We don't have any pre-qualifications for our classes, but we'd like to learn a bit more about you to make sure you'd be a good match.

Once we get your application, we'll set up a short phone interview for later in the week. The interview is an opportunity for us to get to know you, for you to ask questions, and for us to determine if Austin Coding Academy is a good fit.

Within one week after the interview, you'll find out if you've been accepted and we'll follow up with a warm welcome and instructions on how to prepare for your course.
